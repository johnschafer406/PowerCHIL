How CHIL Works

Controller‑Hardware‑in‑the‑Loop (CHIL) connects your live PPC hardware to a high‑fidelity real‑time simulator (Typhoon HIL or RTDS). The simulator emulates transformers, MV feeders, inverters, and grid events with sub‑microsecond resolution.

Setpoints and measurements flow over real protocol links—Modbus, DNP3, IEC‑61850—so the PPC operates exactly as it will on site. We inject voltage steps, frequency ramps, or faults and capture responses at up to 5 kHz.

The result: a digital twin where you can tune PI loops, validate compliance, and ship with confidence—all before the truck rolls.
